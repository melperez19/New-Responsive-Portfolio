About Me

Hi There! Thanks for stopping by my page :). My name is Melissa Perez and I'm 31 years old, born of hispanic descent and raised in Perth Amboy, New Jersey. I gradudated college in 2009 with a BA in Art and Art History and have dabbled in different job titles since then (ex. Cartooning Instructor for kids at the local art gallery, portrait photographer, and archive generalist/ Photoshop editor for an online art and framing company). I've been working in digital printing for a little over 3 years now on the production end creating banners and other promotional products. I love working with computers, files, and layouts and I'm now at the point where I want to move from the production side of helping businesses and focus on building their online presence. I'm currently enrolled in an intensive web development course in hopes of achieving this new professional goal.

Some random facts about me : I'm shy, but friendly and quite hilarious once I come out of my shell. Anyone who knows me knows that I love hats and music. I learned how to play guitar and have explored my inner coolness with DJing. I have a beautiful soon to be 6yr old daughter and an amazing partner who inspires me every day. I'm the youngest of 3 siblings and family is everything.

Please feel free to explore my website and contact me for more information on credentials and possible projects! Thanks :)
